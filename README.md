# Keurig Dr Pepper / Dr Pepper Snapple Group (dr-pepper-snapple-group)

Dr Pepper Snapple Group was an integrated brand owner, manufacturer, and distributor of nonalcoholic beverages in the United States, Canada, Mexico, and the Caribbean. In 2018 the company merged with Keurig Green Mountain to form Keurig Dr Pepper, a leading North American beverage company with a portfolio of more than 150 brands including Dr Pepper, 7UP, Snapple, Canada Dry, Keurig, and Peet's Coffee.
